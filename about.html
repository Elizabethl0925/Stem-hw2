About My Volleyball Journey

How volleyball shaped my skills, mindset, and goals.

How It Started

I remember when I was little my parents put me in a volleyball class with my cousin and at that time I didn't really pay much interests in volleyball. During middle school and mainly the summer of 7th grade, I started to pay more interest in volleyball. I didn't really know how I started to like it but I feel like when I was playing with my friends I was having lots of fun and laughing around. In 8th grade I joined my school volleyball team and I've enjoyed it a lot. I've made new friends from other grades that's on the team and my skills improved a lot because of my amazing coach. During the summertime I would practice with my dad in my backyard to make sure my skills don't fade. That's how my volleyball journey started.

From practices to matches, every experience helped me grow both on and off the court.

What Volleyball Taught Me

Volleyball taught me how to communicate under pressure, stay focused during tough moments, and trust my teammates. I learned that communication, teamwork, and trust between teammate are the most important things because you must be able to communicate with each other on the court even in tough times and you must trust each other's abilities to help the teaam win.

These lessons go beyond sports and apply to school, teamwork, and personal goals.
